Overview

During the pandemic, life became fragmented and emotionally challenging for many. Individuals were juggling multiple tools to track mental health, journal daily experiences, and stay connected with loved ones ‚Äî all at the same time.

This gap inspired Alienpooh: a unified digital platform designed to support emotional well-being, simplify life organization, and foster meaningful connections in a world where physical proximity was not always possible.

As the Product Designer, I led the UX process end-to-end ‚Äî from research and ideation to high-fidelity design and system definition ‚Äî shaping a holistic experience rooted in empathy and emotional resonance.

The Challenge

In a landscape of isolated tools, users were overwhelmed because:

  • Life-management tools were fragmented across different apps
  • No single space combined emotional tracking, journaling, and social connection
  • Users felt disconnected and unsupported during periods of isolation

The core challenge became:

How do we design a unified platform that is intuitive, comprehensive, emotionally supportive, and delightful to use?

Understanding the Users

To ground the product in real needs, I conducted analysis and empathy mapping to uncover deeper insights about users.

  • Users felt overwhelmed managing multiple disconnected tools
  • Many craved emotional closeness with loved ones, even virtually
  • Emotional design was often overlooked in existing solutions

These insights became the foundation of Alienpooh‚Äôs experience, guiding both structural decisions and interaction priorities.

Vision & Ideation

With a grounded understanding of user needs, I defined a clear vision for Alienpooh.

Big Idea

Create a personal digital space where users can:

  • Track mental wellness
  • Maintain meaningful journals
  • Connect emotionally with others

Design Principles

  • Simplicity ‚Äî reduce cognitive load through intuitive workflows
  • Emotional design ‚Äî evoke calm and connection through visuals
  • Flexibility ‚Äî support diverse emotional states and journeys

UX Approach

Market Research & Audit

I analyzed competing platforms to understand how existing solutions addressed life management. The research revealed a clear gap: no product offered an integrated, emotionally supportive environment combining mental well-being, journaling, and social connection.

Wireframes & Prototyping

I translated early concepts into wireframes and low-fidelity prototypes to visualize user flows and validate assumptions. These iterations helped identify friction points early and ensured alignment with user expectations.

Design Execution

  • High-fidelity designs balancing functionality with emotional warmth
  • A reusable design system (typography, color, interactions)
  • Continuous feedback loops with users, stakeholders, and developers

Outcome

  • Emotional support and social connection in a single platform
  • Reduced mental effort by consolidating multiple tools
  • Serene, intuitive, and engaging user experience
  • Scalable system ready for future growth

This project taught me that designing beyond functionality is essential.

Emotional design matters ‚Äî users don‚Äôt just interact with products, they feel them. Rapid iteration and adaptability enabled Alienpooh to evolve into a resilient, human-centered platform prepared for long-term growth.
