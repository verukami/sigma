Product Design Financial Services Design Systems UX Strategy

Redesigning core banking journeys, scaling design systems, and driving product discovery

Role

Product Designer
UX Strategy · Interaction Design · Design Systems

Company

Globant — 2020
Enterprise banking platform

Context

During my time at Globant, I worked on CitiBanamex’s digital banking platform at a moment of transformation. The work spanned three parallel initiatives critical to the product’s evolution.

Workstream 1 — BancaNet Redesign

Challenge

BancaNet’s core journeys had grown fragmented over time, increasing cognitive load and reducing users’ ability to complete tasks efficiently.

Key Decisions

  • Restructured information architecture
  • Redesigned core flows to reduce friction
  • Prioritized clarity and accessibility

Impact

  • Improved usability across core tasks
  • Reduced friction in high-frequency flows

Workstream 2 — Design System Delivery (BlueBox)

Challenge

Multiple teams were designing in parallel without shared standards, resulting in inconsistencies and duplicated effort.

Key Decisions

  • Defined reusable components and patterns
  • Aligned design with development workflows

Impact

  • Increased consistency across products
  • Faster design and development cycles

Workstream 3 — Personalized Product Discovery

Challenge

Users were largely unaware of the breadth of CitiBanamex’s product offering.

Key Decisions

  • Designed contextual discovery modules
  • Embedded components within core journeys

Impact

  • Increased engagement with discovery features
  • Scalable patterns for future growth

This project reinforced the importance of designing at multiple layers simultaneously — from core experience to system foundations and growth.
