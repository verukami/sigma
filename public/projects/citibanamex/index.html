Product Design · Financial Services

CitiBanamex

Redesigning core banking journeys, scaling design systems, and driving product discovery

Context

During my time at Globant, I worked on CitiBanamex’s digital banking platform at a moment of transformation. The work spanned three parallel initiatives critical to the product’s evolution:

  • The redesign of BancaNet, CitiBanamex’s core digital banking platform
  • The definition and delivery of a shared design system across teams
  • The ideation of personalized product discovery components

Each initiative addressed different layers of the product while sharing the same goal: improving clarity, trust, scalability, and user engagement in a regulated financial environment.

Role

Product Designer
UX Strategy · Interaction Design · Design Systems
Globant — 2020

I was responsible for defining UX strategy and interaction patterns across three parallel streams. I worked independently on design decisions while aligning with product, engineering, and other designers to ensure consistency and scalability.

Workstream 1 — BancaNet Redesign

Challenge

BancaNet’s core journeys had grown fragmented over time, increasing cognitive load and reducing users’ ability to understand their financial context and complete tasks efficiently.

Key Decisions

  • Restructured information architecture to simplify navigation
  • Redesigned core flows to reduce friction and errors
  • Prioritized clarity and accessibility to reinforce trust

Impact

  • Improved usability across core banking tasks
  • Reduced friction in high-frequency flows
  • Stronger foundations for future feature growth

Workstream 2 — Design System Delivery (BlueBox)

Challenge

Multiple teams were designing in parallel without shared standards, resulting in inconsistencies and duplicated effort.

Key Decisions

  • Defined reusable components and interaction patterns
  • Ensured scalability across products and teams
  • Aligned design decisions with development workflows

Impact

  • Increased consistency across products
  • Faster design and development cycles
  • Improved design–engineering collaboration

Workstream 3 — Personalized Product Discovery

Challenge

Users were largely unaware of the breadth of CitiBanamex’s product offering, and existing cross-sell mechanisms lacked relevance.

Key Decisions

  • Designed contextual, behavior-driven discovery modules
  • Embedded components within relevant user journeys
  • Built modular patterns reusable across the platform

Impact

  • Increased engagement with discovery features
  • Better visibility of the product ecosystem
  • Scalable patterns for future growth initiatives

Reflection

Working across core experience redesign, system-level foundations, and growth-oriented components reinforced the importance of designing at multiple layers simultaneously.

This project demonstrates how product design can drive usability, scalability, and business impact when decisions are made holistically rather than feature by feature.
